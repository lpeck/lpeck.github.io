Game Instructions

A number will appear in the green box below.

There are 4 widgets below, Spartan Helmet, football, Block S, and basketball. Each are assigned a number between 1 and 12.

Click on a widget to add its value to the running total in the white box.

To win the game reach get the running total in the white box to equal the given number exactly. If your score goes over you lose.
